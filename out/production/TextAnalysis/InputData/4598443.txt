

Abstract-The enterprises which provide express delivery services must have high regard for customer and offer good services to improve the customer service level. As a newly thought of marketing, cross-selling can improve the economic benefits of enterprises and strengthen the relation between enterprise and customer. The model of cross-selling of express delivery business is established by using two data mining algorithms, association rule abstracting algorithm and decision tree classification algorithm, which are used in cross-selling analysis. The actual experiment is done, and the result shows the methods are effecting for express delivery business.

Key Words: Data mining, Express delivery business, Cross-selling
