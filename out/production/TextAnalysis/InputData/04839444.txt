

Abstract?The Field-portable Imaging Radiometric Spectrometer Technology Midwave Extended (FIRST- MWE) system developed by Telops, Inc. was used to capture hyperspectral imagery of turbojet engine exhaust.

The FIRST-MWE is a Michelson interferometer coupled to a 320 x 256 Indium Antimonide (InSb) focal plane array with instantaneous field of view (IFOV) of 0.35 mrad.

Exhaust from a diesel-fueled Turbine Technologies, Ltd.
